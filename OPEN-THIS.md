# The Ultimate Gemini Prompt Library

**20 "One-Shot" Prompts for Agency-Quality AI Images**

Create professional product photos, lifestyle shots, and marketing assets in seconds using Google Gemini's image generation capabilities.

The background is a smooth, gradient [INSERT COLOR - e.g., Deep Ocean Blue]. 8k resolution, highly detailed advertising photography.

## Part 3: Product + People (Contextual Lifestyle)
*Showing how the product fits into real life*

### 15. The "Coffee Shop" Remote Work Setup
**Best for:** Software, laptops, or business tools

```
Look at the attached product images. Generate a realistic photo of a wooden table in a busy, sunlit coffee shop. Placed naturally on the table is [THIS EXACT PRODUCT]. In the slightly blurred background, a [INSERT PERSON DESCRIPTION - e.g., the attached person] is working on a laptop and drinking coffee. The focus is sharp on the product, with a creamy bokeh background.
```

**Example Result:** Your software interface or product on a table, with a blurry "digital nomad" working behind it.

## Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific** with colors, lighting, and styling preferences
- **Upload high-quality** product photos for better recognition
- **Iterate quickly** - if the first result isn't perfect, adjust the descriptive elements
- **Mix and match** - combine elements from different prompts to create your unique style
